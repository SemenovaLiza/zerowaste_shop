About RainyDay
The history of our store began with the association of 8 volunteers who were not indifferent to the fate of our planet. Now our team is growing and we are cooperating with various foundations, ranging from foundations where clothes are handed over for recycling, ending with a foundation that grows bamboo, from which we produce our home and personal care products. What Zero Waste is?
Zero Waste is a concept of production and consumption leading to a reduction in the amount of waste. In zero waste stores you can buy cosmetics in solid form and without packaging. This will reduce the use of resources for packaging and less garbage is generated after using the product. A few more words about solid products — these are the products of the future. All the advantages of the Zero Waste concept in them are realized in the absolute. Much less packaging materials are required for transportation than for products in containers. Storage space is needed, on average, two times less. It is also a convenience in traveling.
