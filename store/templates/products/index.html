About RainyDay
The history of our store began with the association of 8 volunteers who were not indifferent to the fate of our planet. Now our team is growing and we are cooperating with various foundations, ranging from foundations where clothes are handed over for recycling, ending with a foundation that grows bamboo, from which we produce our home and personal care products. You can also take part in the development of our store, you can learn more about this in the "delivery" section.
